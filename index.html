USA Population Interactive Map 2020

This is a map uses a color gradient to represent the population levels of each state, with different shades indicating varying population sizes which is based on the US census 2020 data. States with a darker color likely have a higher population, whereas those with a lighter color have a lower population. The map includes an information box, likely shown when a user hovers over or clicks on a state displaying the name of the state and its corresponding population figure based on the 2020 Census data. There's also a legend on the right side that correlates the color shades with the population figures, indicating the scale from low to high. Besides, you can zoom the map if you want.

 USA Population Line Race Chart in Four Main Regions

This is an animated line chart shows the population growth race in four main parts of the USA which are the NorthEast Region, South Region, Midwest Region, and West Region. The chart reveals the growth trend of the population from 1910-2020 while hovering the mouse on the chart you can visualize the exact population of that year for each population. The replay button on the right enables you to replay the animation if you want. Also, as you can see from the drop down menu that you can also view the animation of population growth for different areas.
